Answers

When was Steve Jobs born?
Steve Jobs was born on February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, California. He was adopted shortly after his birth by Paul and Clara Jobs,
and grew up in Mountain View, California. From an early age, Jobs demonstrated an interest in electronics and technology.
What was Steve Jobs's educational background?
Steve Jobs attended Homestead High School in Cupertino, California and briefly attended Reed College in Portland, Oregon before dropping out
after just one semester. Jobs continued to take classes in subjects that interested him, such as calligraphy, and attended lectures at local universities even though he was not formally enrolled.
When did Steve Jobs co-found Apple?
Steve Jobs co-founded Apple Computer, along with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne, in 1976. The company was created in Jobs' parents' garage in
Los Altos, California, and the first Apple computer, the Apple I, was released later that year.
What were some of Steve Jobs's notable innovations at Apple?
Steve Jobs oversaw the development of the Macintosh computer, which introduced the graphical user interface and mouse to personal computing, and
the iPod, which revolutionized the music industry. Jobs also played a significant role in the development of the iPhone, which transformed the mobile phone industry, and the iPad, which created a new category of portable computing.
What was Steve Jobs's role at Apple?
Steve Jobs co-founded Apple Inc. and served as its CEO from 1997 until his resignation in 2011, just a few months before his death. During his tenure,
Jobs was known for his strong leadership and visionary approach to product development, and he played a key role in many of Apple's most significant innovations.
When did Steve Jobs return to Apple?
Steve Jobs returned to Apple in 1997. At the time, Apple was struggling and had lost its position as a leader in the technology industry. Jobs was
brought back to the company as part of its acquisition of NeXT, a computer company that Jobs had founded after leaving Apple in 1985.
What was Steve Jobs's impact on the technology industry?
Steve Jobs had a profound impact on the technology industry, shaping it in ways that are still felt today. His innovative approach to product
design and development, and his emphasis on creating technology that was both functional and beautiful, helped to transform the way that people use and interact with technology.
When did Steve Jobs pass away and from what?
Steve Jobs passed away on October 5, 2011, at the age of 56. He had been battling pancreatic cancer for several years, and had taken several
leaves of absence from his role as CEO of Apple due to his health. Jobs' death was widely mourned in the technology industry and beyond, and he is remembered as one of the most important and influential figures in the history of computing.
