About Me

I go by Leo and I am a creative individual with a passion for art and design. Whether it's through painting, graphic design, or sewing, I am always looking for new ways to express myself and bring my ideas to life. While taking this course in Web Design, I learned a variety of web development technologies, techniques, and methods. I gained valuable knowledge in creating and structuring web pages using HTML, designing layouts and styles using CSS, and implementing interactive features using forms. Moreover, I learned how to improve user experience through responsive design and creating a site that is cohesive, user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing with the help of W3 styles and templates.

As the digital world continues to grow, I believe that having a strong understanding of web development is crucial for any creative individual. The skills and knowledge that I gained in this class will help me achieve my professional goals by enabling me to design and create compelling web pages that showcase my creativity and design skills. Furthermore, these skills will enable me to collaborate more effectively with other designers and developers, leading to more successful projects and opportunities. I am excited to continue my journey in the world of web development and explore new technologies and techniques that will allow me to bring my ideas to life on the web.
